What We Do

JAT BOTS mvp to produce bots using the best development practices and services to the client. The current case study is a artist private lesson booking bot to automate the booking process saving both student and teacher valuable time in connecting to schedule an appointment.

Take the bot for a test spin and check hours of operation and schedule a cello lesson. Click the link in the menu and view your appointment scheduled on the calendar. The bot can be customized to meet the needs of the client and development includes integrating the dialogflow ai to website and social sites. For the Enterprise client RASA with python solutions are available.
